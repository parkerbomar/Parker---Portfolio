About

What would you want in a neighborhood restaurant? We think it should be family-friendly, community-minded and have great food at a fair price. Add to great food and friendly service the feeling that no matter where you’re from, you’re right at home. Welcome to Village Burger!

But don’t let the casual atmosphere fool you, when it comes to food, we mean business. The Village Burger menu is sure to please a wide range of tastes. We serve the classics that you love, cooked with only the freshest ingredients. Top that with frozen custard, a smooth and rich gourmet ice cream treat, and you’re starting to understand what Village Burger is all about.

Atmosphere

Want to stay longer? Relax on one of our covered patios, order beer or wine (or maybe another custard!), and spend some time with friends and family.

The Village Burger golden rule is Be Nice. You'll see it displayed in the restaurant and in the actions and attitudes of all of our employees. So come in, sit down and enjoy the food and fun. We're glad you're here!
